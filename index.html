About

I'm a geospatial analyst and AI builder based in Nairobi, Kenya. I founded Outgrow to bring precision agriculture intelligence to smallholder farmers using channels that already work — USSD, IVR, WhatsApp. I work as a GIS Consultant at Lima Labs, processing LiDAR drone surveys and multispectral imagery for precision agriculture clients across Kenya. I'm pursuing an MSc in GIS at Clark University and hold a BSc in Urban & Regional Planning from Technical University of Kenya, plus certificates from ALX (Software Engineering) and NairoBits Trust (Web Development & UX).

Outside of work I run civic tech through the Mtaa Safi Initiative and Wogi Voices, a women's empowerment project. I've trained 1,500+ students across Kenya and Uganda.
